# Book Outlines

This folder holds current working structure for the playbook. These artifacts guide development, but they are not publishable manuscript unless explicitly promoted into `book/manuscript/`.

## Artifact Types

- `outline`: section or book-level conceptual structure, chapter sequence, and governing moves.
- `granular`: sub-beat structure for a section, chapter, or major module, including doctrine phrases and open decisions.
- `matrix`: modular or branching structure where a linear chapter must also show audience, gate, skill, or signal configurations.
- `narrative`: continuous prose used to validate whether the structure creates reader-pull. Narrative drafts belong here until they are deliberately revised into manuscript.
- `end-to-end`: cross-section view of the full playbook, including threads that need to land across multiple sections.

## Working Sequence

For major reader-facing structure work, default to this sequence:

1. Conceptual frame.
2. Granular sub-beats.
3. Narrative validation.

Use a matrix alongside that sequence when modularity or audience branching needs to stay visible. Do not treat a narrative validation draft as final prose; use it to test felt need, continuity, return, and whether a reader has a reason to keep moving.
