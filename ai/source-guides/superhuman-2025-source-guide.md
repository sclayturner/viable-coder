## Translation Notes

The main conceptual reframe is:

- **Old frame**: become "superhuman" by mastering AI agents to exceed personal limits.
- **Current frame**: become and remain viable by using AI while preserving human origination, answerability, judgment, review capacity, and long-term condition.

Treat "superhuman" language as historical. The durable idea is not that AI makes the person superhuman. The durable idea is that AI can widen human capability only if it does not hollow out the human faculties that make the capability worth having.

Treat "Orchestrator" language as a precursor. In current doctrine, orchestration must be governed by gates, signals, skills, and the human holding the two ends.

Treat "personal limits" language as a precursor to current condition, observability, upkeep, operating profile, and nine-dimension viability work.
