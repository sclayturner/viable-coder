# Eval: Voice

Check substantial prose against these questions:

- Does this sound like an experienced builder speaking from earned responsibility?
- Does it preserve the lived human source beneath the founder and engineering claims?
- Does it avoid generic AI productivity language?
- Does it preserve the tension between opportunity and risk?
- Does it keep the human answerable?
- Does it use concrete reader situations where abstraction would be weak?
- Where the passage is structural, does it still carry the cost of error, ownership, time, or consequence?
- If it uses Clay's painful scarcity period, does it show capability formed under constraint without romanticizing pain?
- Does it avoid overexplaining what a capable reader can infer?
