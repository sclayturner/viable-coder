# Workflow: Draft Chapter

1. Read root `AGENTS.md` and `book/AGENTS.md`.
2. Read the relevant section outline in `book/outlines/`.
3. Read only the doctrine and research files needed for the chapter.
4. Draft from the reader's situation toward a practical distinction, diagnostic, or move.
5. Keep research support out of the main prose unless it changes the reader's action.
6. Run `ai/evals/voice-checklist.md`, `ai/evals/structure-checklist.md`, and `ai/evals/doctrine-drift-checklist.md`.
