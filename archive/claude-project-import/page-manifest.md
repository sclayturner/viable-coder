# Viable Coder Page Manifest
 
This manifest describes the purpose of each public HTML page in the Viable Coder site and flags pages where current content is partly placeholder, planned, or future-facing.
 
## Site-Level Purpose
 
Viable Coder exists to help people move from AI-assisted "vibe coding" to durable AI-native product ownership, while staying honest about what must remain viable over time: the practitioner, the practice, and the conditions that make the practice possible.
 
The site has two primary halves:
 
- **Becoming Viable**: practical mentorship, resources, and doctrine for people and companies trying to build real AI-native software capability now.
- **Remaining Viable**: a public inquiry into whether that way of working holds up across cognition, judgment, identity, economics, ethics, and time.
## Page Manifest
 
### `index.html` — Home
 
**Purpose:** Establish the core thesis of the site: moving from vibe coding to viable coding means both adopting AI-native product ownership and asking whether the practice remains viable over time.
 
**Role in the site:** The conceptual front door. It introduces the two-sided structure, routes visitors toward Becoming Viable or Remaining Viable, and frames the nine dimensions as the long-term credibility layer behind the work.
 
**Caveats:** None. This page appears structurally complete.
 
### `becoming-viable.html` — Becoming Viable
 
**Purpose:** Define the six audience situations that bring people to the practical side of the work.
 
**Role in the site:** The sorting page for commercial and applied visitors. It helps founders, leaders, teams, and emerging builders recognize their situation before choosing mentorship or self-directed resources.
 
**Primary audiences represented:**
 
- Internal Talent Worth Developing
- SaaS Vendor Dependency
- External Dev Dependency
- Legacy Software Debt
- AI Development Gap
- Prototype-to-Production Wall
**Caveats:** None. This page appears structurally complete.

**Purpose:** Present the paid engagement: executive-level mentorship that transfers real AI-native product ownership into a company.
