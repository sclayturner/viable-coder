# Narrative Ledger

This working ledger tracks recurring scenes, questions, and callbacks for the playbook. It is not lesson content prose. Use it to keep literary structure intentional as outlines, lessons, modules, and exercises develop.

The governing rhythm is defined in `doctrine/reader-orientation-pattern.md`: Scene -> Tension -> Distinction -> Tool -> Return.

## Use Notes

- Add an anchor when a scene, question, or callback is likely to recur across units.
- Keep anchors tied to doctrine, tools, modules, or signals. Do not track decorative anecdotes.
- Update the likely owner when a unit outline changes.
- If an anchor becomes canonical terminology, update `doctrine/playbook-lexicon.md`.
