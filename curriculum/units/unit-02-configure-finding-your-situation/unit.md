**Structural note on this revision.** The earlier seven-lesson draft (now superseded) opened on three lessons of meta-commentary about how the unit worked before the builder met themselves — violating the felt-need rhythm Unit 1 established and failing to honor the explicit handoff at the end of Unit 1. This revision lands the builder in recognition immediately. The six-lesson spine is **Find Your Situation → The Check → Build It, or Borrow It → The Floor → Your Kit Isn't a Cage → Where to Start Today.** The reframe (*every escape is a trade*) is earned inside Lesson 1 as the unit's lead distinction rather than installed as a separate move ahead of the situations. The check follows recognition rather than gating it — the kit confirms behaviorally what the builder has already seen in a scene. The five universals are named compactly in Lesson 4 as operating conditions, not as a recap of Unit 1.

The narrative draft at `curriculum/outlines/unit-2-narrative.md` was the first pass against this standard; the lesson content here is the rewrite and supersedes the narrative as the authoritative Unit 2 prose. The outline at `curriculum/outlines/unit-2-outline.md` is the structural map of this lesson content.
