Research names what is worth measuring. Data measures it. Both rest on a standing commitment that makes their pairing honest.

Standing Commitment

Unfavorable findings will be published with the same visibility as favorable ones.

Metrics are locked with the PhD collaborator before they can be gamed, and before any of them go public. Methodology changes are versioned and visible. The same commitment appears in full on Data — both sides of the inquiry stand behind it.
