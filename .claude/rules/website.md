# Website Instructions

Use this folder for the public website and its supporting documentation.

## Working Rules

- `website/public/` is the current static site surface.
- `website/docs/page-manifest.md` explains page intent and readiness.
- `website/docs/data-collection-alignment.md` explains the gap between mocked public findings and real instrumentation.
- Do not treat website copy as canonical doctrine unless it has also been promoted to `doctrine/`.
- Do not let the website overpromise book availability, research maturity, or mentorship capacity.

## Public-Facing Guardrails

- Becoming Viable is the practical and commercial side.
- Remaining Viable is the inquiry and credibility side.
- The data surface should not become a sales surface.
- The playbook page may describe the product direction, but should stay honest about its development status.
