# Research Instructions

Use this folder for research streams, instruments, evidence ledgers, and measurement design.

## Working Rules

- Separate evidence, interpretation, and doctrine.
- Preserve uncertainty. Mark claims as supported, inferred, speculative, or open when needed.
- Do not overstate mocked website data as live research evidence.
- Use `research/evidence-ledgers/ai-watcher.md` and `website/docs/data-collection-alignment.md` when discussing current measurement capability.
- Store durable research streams in `research/streams/`.
- Store repeatable survey, benchmark, journal, or scoring instruments in `research/instruments/`.

## Evidence Standard

Research material should help answer one of these questions:

- What is under threat?
- What is observable?
- What is measured now?
- What still requires an asked instrument, passive data source, or analysis-time enrichment?
- How does the evidence change the playbook's practical guidance?
