# Doctrine Instructions

Use this folder for durable concepts that govern the kit curriculum, private kit app, and public website.

## Canonical Doctrine

- `value-proposition.md`: purpose, market position, and mission.
- `playbook-lexicon.md`: terms that should remain consistent across the project.
- `audiences.md`: the canonical six audiences (situations) the kit and website serve; the source of truth every downstream artifact defers to.
- `organic-intelligence.md`: the human side of the human-machine distinction.
- `human-condition.md`, `observability.md`, `upkeep.md`: the maintenance trilogy for the human as an instrument.
- `nine-dimensions-model.md`: long-term viability model.
- `reader-orientation-pattern.md`: the lesson- and module-level teaching rhythm for orienting builders through scene, tension, distinction, tool, and return.
- `voice/`: source material for Clay's style, posture, lived answerability, and philosophical commitments.

## Working Rules

- Change doctrine deliberately. A doctrine change may require follow-up edits in curriculum, website, research, and skills.
- When adding a term, decide whether it belongs in `playbook-lexicon.md`.
- When changing a core distinction, check whether the four-unit architecture still holds.
- Keep doctrine reusable. If a passage is lesson prose, move it to `curriculum/`.
- When using personal source material, keep it structurally relevant. It should explain why a doctrine, gate, module, or discipline matters; it should not become decorative memoir.
- When changing how the kit curriculum teaches, check `reader-orientation-pattern.md` and decide whether `playbook-lexicon.md`, `curriculum/AGENTS.md`, skills, agents, or evals need matching updates.
- **Kit operationalizability.** The playbook ships as a private kit app with integrated curriculum, working agents, exercises, telemetry, and dashboards. A doctrinal claim the kit cannot operationalize is a doctrinal claim not yet ready to ratify. When changing load-bearing doctrine (nine dimensions, ADLC gates, audience model, maintenance trilogy, signal set), flag the kit implication and consult `ai/viable-coder-kit-concept-manifest.md` for the coupling contract.
